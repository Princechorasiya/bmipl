our Products

pp bags

Pp bags

description

Polypropylene bags, also referred to as PP bags, are a type of thermoplastic resin material derived from the polymerization of propylene. Woven polypropylene comprises polypropylene strips or threads woven in both warp and weft directions, resulting in a lightweight yet durable and robust material.

benefits

Polypropylene bags are versatile, durable, and eco-friendly, ideal for various applications. They are non-toxic, stain-resistant, and easy to clean. Lightweight and reusable, they offer options for breathability or waterproofing and withstand high temperatures and chemicals effectively.

wpp/lpp/ad*star bags

wpp/lpp/ad*star bags

description

We provide PP woven sacks manufactured without adhesives, employing heat-welding techniques on the fabric. These sacks integrate the strengths of traditional types like paper, PE, and PP, yielding a superior product.

benefits

Our durable sacks boast minimal bursting, resist humidity, and feature a convenient valve for easy filling. They are eco-friendly, cost-effective, recyclable, and reusable, with vibrant, non-fading printing. Hermetically sealed, they withstand rough handling, making them ideal for export use.

bopp bags

bopp bags

description

Crafted from top-tier Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) films, our bags exhibit exceptional tensile strength, dimensional stability, waterproofing, and transparency. BOPP Printed Bags offer expanded branding options, empowering our clients to attract buyers effectively.

benefits

Our BOPP bags offer superior strength and durability, reducing overall costs with their lightweight design and resistance to breakage. Moisture-proof and recyclable, they feature high gloss finishes, UV inhibitors, and scratch-resistant printing, ideal for marketing in retail.

biodegradable bags

biodegradable bags

description

Biodegradable woven bags are sustainable alternatives to traditional plastic bags. Made from natural fibers, they decompose over time, reducing environmental harm and promoting eco-conscious packaging solutions.

benefits

Why biodegradable woven bags? The answer is for lessening global warming impact. Biodegradable woven bags combat global warming by addressing plastic pollution and reducing CO2 emissions through eco-friendly decomposition. They support sustainable practices and promote a circular economy for a greener future.

fibc products

fibc products

description

FIBCs, also known as bulk bags or big bags, are versatile and efficient containers used across various industries for storing and transporting bulk materials. These large, flexible bags are made from woven polypropylene or other durable fabrics and are designed to handle heavy loads ranging from hundreds to several thousand kilograms.

benefits

Key benefits of FIBCs include their ability to maximize storage space, facilitate efficient handling and transportation, and reduce packaging waste compared to traditional methods. They are designed for strength, durability, and ease of use, meeting stringent safety and quality standards for reliable performance in diverse industrial environments.
