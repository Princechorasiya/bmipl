introduction

Our Group is made up of two companies, JK Sons Engineers Pvt. Ltd. (JKSEPL) and Bilaspur Mining Industries Pvt. Ltd. (BMIPL), both of which are based in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. We began our journey in 2008 with a traditional stitched bag unit capable of producing 3600 MT per year. As rightly said “Every big castle was once started with a single block; despise no small beginnings. A little step taken every day builds up the hope of greater accomplishments. Do something every day!”


With the goal of becoming the world's most efficient and sustainable packaging solution provider , we expanded in 2012 and successfully installed our first fully integrated block bottom plant from Starlinger, Austria.In the last ten years, we have successfully centralized five plants in our two manufacturing units i.e. JK Sons and Bilaspur Mining, using our own logistics fleets. Our plants are fully equipped with in-house quality testing and storage. JKSEPL and BMIPL, with a combined installed capacity of 600 million bags per year, are India's largest manufacturers of woven Block Bottom bags.


Furthermore, BMIPL is undergoing an expansion to enhance its capacity, and our goal is to become the world's leading manufacturer of Woven Block Bottom bags. JKSEPL and BMIPL have a reputation for producing cement industry bags, and their clients include the world's top ten cement producers. As of now, they extrude nearly 36000MT of Raffia .


Our goal is to assist each client in creating a better future. We combine technical expertise and business intelligence to catalyze change and maximize returns. Today, our brand evaluation is defined by our global consumer experience.

JP Agarwal
CHAIRMAN AND MD

JP Agarwal, one of the company's founders, has over 41 years of industry experience and serves as its Chairman and Managing Director. He built JKSEPL and BMIPL from the scratch, and with his in-depth understanding of the business and its process. He has transitioned the companies into India's leading manufacturer of PP Block Bottom Bags. He is intimately involved in project setup, corporate planning, business development, and budgeting. He is the visionary behind JK Sons and Bilaspur Mining because of his excellent communication and people management skills.
